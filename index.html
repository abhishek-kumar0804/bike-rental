Ladakh

Ladakh, also known as “India’s Own Moon land” because of its beauty, is a union territory in Kashmir. Ladakh extends from the Siachen Glacier to the main great Himalayas and its beauty is incomparable. It is one of the highest regions in the world. The natural features of Ladakh include high plains and deep valleys. The main tourist sites here include Tibetan Buddhism and spellbinding landscapes. Ladakh not only offers beautiful and serene monasteries but also mesmerizing rich natural beauty. The natural beauty of Ladakh hits so hard that one wishes to explore it more. There are a lot of places to visit in Ladakh but some places need an inner permit. Ladakh is an amazing destination with lots of secrets in it. It is the ideal destination to spend your vacations in peace.
